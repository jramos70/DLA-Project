Welcome to the space adventure game!

In this game, you will embark on a journey to the outskirts of space. As captain of the ship, you will lead your space crew go to the coordinates marked on your map, and drop off the supplies to the intergalactic space station.

However, it is a very dangerous journey as there are many space pirates, asteroids, and other treacherous obstacles in your path. If you are ready to embark on this journey, press start!
